## General high-severity
- **CISA KEV additions (07 Jul 2026)** — [CRITICAL] due date 10 Jul, [CISA alert](https://www.cisa.gov/news-events/alerts/2026/07/07/cisa-adds-three-known-exploited-vulnerabilities-catalog).
  - **CVE-2026-55255 — Langflow IDOR (Authorization Bypass Through User-Controlled Key)** — CVSS 9.9. `/api/v1/responses` resolves a flow by UUID with no owner check; any authenticated user can execute another user's flow. Fixed in 1.9.2. Langflow is now the second time this quarter it's hit KEV (CVE-2025-3248 was the JADEPUFFER agentic-ransomware entry point). Details: [Sysdig writeup](https://www.sysdig.com/blog/understanding-langflow-cve-2026-55255-and-why-higher-cvss-vulnerabilities-arent-always-the-most-exploited), [GHSA-qrpv-q767-xqq2](https://github.com/advisories/GHSA-qrpv-q767-xqq2).
  - **CVE-2026-48908 — JoomShaper SP Page Builder unrestricted upload of dangerous file type** — CVE-2026-56290 — Joomlack Page Builder improper access control. Both are Joomla ecosystem site-takeover primitives; ubiquitous on small marketing/microsite hosts.
- **Adobe ColdFusion CVE-2026-48282 exploited in the wild** — [CRITICAL] CVSS 10.0. RDS path traversal → unauthenticated RCE, patched 30 Jun, exploitation began <2h after public details on 02 Jul; fresh reporting 07 Jul confirms ongoing attacks from 103.207.14.220 (India). Only exploitable if RDS is on and its auth is off (both non-default) but that pattern is common in dev boxes exposed to the Internet. Patch: 2025 Update 10 / 2023 Update 21. Sources: [BleepingComputer](https://www.bleepingcomputer.com/news/security/max-severity-adobe-coldfusion-flaw-now-exploited-in-attacks/) (07 Jul), [Help Net Security](https://www.helpnetsecurity.com/2026/07/07/adobe-coldfusion-cve-2026-48282-exploitation-detected/) (07 Jul), [Security Affairs](https://securityaffairs.com/194837/hacking/adobe-coldfusion-flaw-cve-2026-48282-now-exploited-in-the-wild.html) (07 Jul).
- **Umbraco delivery-API vulnerability — patched 07 Jul 2026** — [HIGH]. Affects Umbraco 13, 17 and 18. Allows unauthenticated retrieval of protected content when it's included via output expansion on an unprotected document. Broadly deployed CMS; watch for scanner activity within days. Source: [Umbraco advisory](https://umbraco.com/blog/security-advisory-july-7-2026-security-patches-are-now-available/).
- **Union County (OH) reportedly paid ~$1M to Kairos extortion group** — [NOTABLE]. Fresh coverage 07 Jul says the county paid to keep the May 2025 stolen data (45,487 individuals, incl. SSNs, passports, fingerprint and medical info) offline. Notable because Kairos runs data-theft extortion without deploying encryption — the model that's steadily replacing classic ransomware among mid-sized US local-gov targets. Source: [SecurityWeek](https://www.securityweek.com/county-government-reportedly-paid-1-million-to-cyber-extortion-group/).
- **Spanish National Police + FBI arrest of alleged Cyber Army of Russia Reborn (Z-Pentest) operator announced 07 Jul** — [NOTABLE]. Arrest itself was in March in Palencia; publicly disclosed Monday. Suspect accused of coordinating attacks via encrypted apps and exfiltrating a Ukrainian associate to Russia via Poland/Belarus. Part of international Operation Riptide.
