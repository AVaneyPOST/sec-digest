## General high-severity
- **Exim 4.99.4 released — CVE-2026-48840, PROXY-protocol pre-auth info leak.** Versions 4.88–4.99.3 with `SUPPORT_PROXY` compiled in and a non-empty `hosts_proxy` mishandle short PROXYv2 frames: TCPv6/length=0 leaks 16 bytes, TCPv4 with length<12 leaks 4 bytes, of uninitialized stack into the SMTP banner. Useful for ASLR/stack-layout disclosure in pre-auth chains against MTAs. CVSS 5.3. [exim.org release notes via DirectAdmin forum](https://forum.directadmin.com/threads/exim-4-99-4-security-release.82300/), [linuxcompatible.org write-up](https://www.linuxcompatible.org/story/exim-4994-addresses-proxyprotocol-vulnerability) — 2026-05-30 — NOTABLE.
- **Palo Alto Networks CVE-2026-0257 advisory updated; exploitation continues.** GlobalProtect portal/gateway auth bypass via forged authentication-override cookies; Rapid7 reports attackers chaining with an unpatched RCE zero-day for full firewall takeover. CISA KEV due date for federal agencies: 2026-06-19. The advisory update bringing fresh exploitation confirmation was 2026-05-29 — sits at the very edge of the window. [Palo Alto advisory](https://security.paloaltonetworks.com/CVE-2026-0257), [Rapid7 ETR](https://www.rapid7.com/blog/post/etr-rapid7-observed-exploitation-of-pan-os-globalprotect-authentication-bypass-vulnerability-cve-2026-0257/) — 2026-05-29 update — HIGH.

Fourth major Linux LPE in ~30 days (Copy Fail → Dirty Frag → Fragnesia → CIFSwitch). [BleepingComputer](https://www.bleepingcomputer.com/news/security/new-cifswitch-linux-flaw-gives-root-on-multiple-distributions/), [SecurityOnline PoC details](https://securityonline.info/cifswitch-local-root-exploit-poc/).

[Rapid7](https://www.rapid7.com/blog/post/ve-authenticated-rce-via-argument-injection-gogs-unfixed/), [SecurityWeek](https://www.securityweek.com/gogs-zero-day-exposes-servers-to-remote-code-execution/).

XIQ-native tokens and OAuth/JWT unaffected. CVSS 6.3. Sources disagree on publication date (2026-05-29 vs 2026-05-30); see Notes. [TheHackerWire](https://www.thehackerwire.com/vulnerability/CVE-2026-9831/), [OffSeq](https://radar.offseq.com/threat/cve-2026-9831-cwe-362-concurrent-execution-using-s-84a029a5).
