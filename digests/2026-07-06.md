- **Bad Epoll (CVE-2026-46242) — Linux kernel LPE, root from unprivileged
  user, works on Android.** Disclosed 2026-07-03 → 2026-07-04, outside
  today's window. Use-after-free race in `epoll` (introduced by
  `58c9b016e128` in 2023-04-08, fixed by `a6dc643c6931` on 2026-04-24)
  with a ~six-instruction race window. One of ~10 candidate Android-root
  Linux kernel bugs out of the ~130 exploited on Google kernelCTF this
  cycle. Reported and exploited by Jaeyoung Chung as a kernelCTF 0-day
  submission. Distributions on v6.4+ that have not backported the
  April 24 patch are still vulnerable — audit any Linux touching
  telecom RAN / edge / core, and any Android-based COTS handset fleet.
  [The Hacker News](https://thehackernews.com/2026/07/new-bad-epoll-linux-kernel-flaw-lets.html)
  · [J-jaeyoung/bad-epoll (GitHub PoC)](https://github.com/J-jaeyoung/bad-epoll)
  · [Cybersecurity News](https://cybersecuritynews.com/bad-epoll-0-day-vulnerability/)
