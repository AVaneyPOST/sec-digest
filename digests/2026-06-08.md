## Telecom

- **No fresh telecom-protocol or operator disclosure in the 24h window.** Carry-overs from the previous 48-72h that remain operationally hot:
  - **Cisco Catalyst SD-WAN Manager — CVE-2026-20245 — still actively exploited, still no patch.** Authenticated `netadmin` → crafted-file upload → arbitrary command execution as **root** on the management plane controlling every vEdge / cEdge in the fabric. Cisco PSIRT confirmed in-the-wild exploitation pushing configuration changes to edge devices — i.e., the attacker is using the platform exactly the way the platform is designed to be used. Chains with CVE-2026-20182 (CVSS 10.0 unauth auth-bypass on the SD-WAN Controller, KEV'd, FCEB deadline expired 2026-05-17) and CVE-2026-20127 to give an external attacker the `netadmin` foothold that the new CVE then weaponises. **Why a telco offensive-security practitioner cares:** SD-WAN Manager is the single pane of glass for branch / MPLS-replacement deployments many operators sell as a managed-service line — root there = control of every customer fabric and every operator-managed CPE registered to it. Mitigation: restrict management-plane access to a jump host, monitor for unexpected NETCONF activity / new SSH keys, track Talos's UAT-8616 indicators. Advisory 2026-06-04. ## General high-severity

- **DentaQuest 234 GB / 2.6 M-record dump landed on ShinyHunters DLS over 2026-06-05/06.** DentaQuest is the largest US Medicaid dental-benefits administrator; the leaked corpus reportedly includes full names, dates of birth, email and physical addresses, phone numbers, gender, **government-issued IDs (passports / driver's licences), health insurance information, and Medicaid IDs** — i.e., the entire identity-fraud kit per record. Attack pattern matches the now-standard ShinyHunters cloud-credential play: phishing-harvested OAuth tokens / app passwords → quiet exfiltration from the cloud storage tier with no on-host malware → extortion → bulk publication when payment fails. Same operator that just dumped Charter (4.9 M) and Carnival (5.99 M) and that breached Instructure Canvas (30 M students/staff). HIPAA Breach Portal entry expected; class-action surface area is essentially the whole adult Medicaid-dental population. Confirmed by DentaQuest 2026-06-01; full leak in-window.
