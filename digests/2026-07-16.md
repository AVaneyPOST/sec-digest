## General high-severity
- **SonicWall SMA1000 CVE-2026-15409 (CVSS 10.0, unauth SSRF → localhost tunnel) + CVE-2026-15410 (CVSS 7.2, post-auth root code injection via `remove_hotfix` path traversal) — chained for full appliance takeover, active in-the-wild exploitation confirmed by Rapid7 MDR pre-disclosure** — [CRITICAL] SonicWall advisory shipped 14 Jul; Rapid7 published detailed telemetry and detection guidance 15 Jul, disclosing pre-advisory exploitation observed on internet-facing SMA 1000 appliances. Attackers used the appliance as a stealthy initial access vector, then locally harvested credentials, active session databases, and TOTP MFA seed configurations to survive network-level remediation. Affects SMA 1000 series (models 6210, 7210, 8200v); fixed in 12.4.3-03453 and 12.5.0-02835. CISA added both CVEs to KEV; BOD 26-04 mitigation deadline for federal agencies is 17 Jul (tomorrow). Any SMA 1000 estate must assume compromise, snapshot for IR, rotate MFA seeds and credentials as part of remediation — not just apply the hotfix. Sources: [SonicWall advisory](https://www.sonicwall.com/support/notices/product-notice-sma-1000-series-affected-by-multiple-vulnerabilities/kA1VN000001nv6D0AQ), [Rapid7 MDR blog (15 Jul)](https://www.rapid7.com/blog/post/etr-rapid7-mdr-team-discovers-new-sonicwall-sma1000-zero-days-being-actively-exploited-cve-2026-15409-cve-2026-15410/), [BleepingComputer](https://www.bleepingcomputer.com/news/security/sonicwall-warns-of-sma1000-flaws-exploited-in-zero-day-attacks-patch-now/), [The Hacker News](https://thehackernews.com/2026/07/two-sonicwall-sma-1000-zero-days.html), [SecurityWeek](https://www.securityweek.com/sonicwall-issues-urgent-sma-patch-warning-for-two-zero-day-exploits/), [Help Net Security](https://www.helpnetsecurity.com/2026/07/14/sonicwall-sma-attacks-via-cve-2026-15409-cve-2026-15410/), [Sophos analysis](https://www.sophos.com/en-us/blog/sonicwall-sma1000-vulnerabilities-in-active-exploitation), [CSA Singapore advisory](https://www.csa.gov.sg/alerts-and-advisories/alerts/al-2026-088/).
